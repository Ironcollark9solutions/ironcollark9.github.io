About Us

Iron Collar K9 Solutions was founded to bring real-world obedience and behavior solutions to hardworking dog owners. We specialize in large, working, and dominant breeds. Our mission is to create confident handlers and reliable dogs, trained in real-world environments by a trainer who understands blue-collar life and real responsibility.

Our Programs

Bronze Fang

Introductory obedience for basic manners, leash skills, and calm behavior. Great for beginners and young dogs.

Iron Grip

Structured obedience under distraction, leash control, and reliable commands with increased focus and impulse control.

Steel Mind

Advanced off-leash control, focus under heavy distraction, and real-world obedience across different environments.

Titan K9 Protocol

Elite-level training for tactical obedience, full off-leash freedom, and control in extreme scenarios.
